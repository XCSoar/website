About XCSoar

XCSoar is a tactical glide computer that provides glider pilots with comprehensive flight planning, navigation, and analysis tools. It features a detailed moving map with terrain, topography, and airspace visualization, real-time flight calculations, thermal assistance, FLARM traffic integration, and extensive task planning capabilities.

Whether you're flying cross-country, competing in competitions, or training, XCSoar offers the tools you need to make informed decisions in the air. The software runs on Android devices, Windows, and Unix systems, making it accessible on a wide range of hardware platforms.
