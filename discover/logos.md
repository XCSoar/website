## XCSoar website logos

The **navbar** uses the square **mark** SVG plus the word **XCSoar** as live text (DejaVu Sans bold; blue/black, red `#d01717`, or green `#2a8033` by theme). Reference SVGs derived from the app [`title.svg`](https://github.com/XCSoar/XCSoar/blob/master/Data/graphics/title.svg) are still in the repo: [`title-navbar.svg`](/img/title-navbar.svg), [`title-red-navbar.svg`](/img/title-red-navbar.svg). Marks: [`xcsoar.svg`](/img/xcsoar.svg), [`xcsoar-red.svg`](/img/xcsoar-red.svg), [`logo-icon-green.svg`](/img/logo-icon-green.svg). The full horizontal composite [`logo-green.svg`](/img/logo-green.svg) remains for other uses.
